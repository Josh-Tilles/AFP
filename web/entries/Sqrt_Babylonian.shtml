Computing Square Roots using the Babylonian Method

Title: Computing Square Roots using the Babylonian Method
Author: René Thiemann (rene /dot/ thiemann /at/ uibk /dot/ ac /dot/ at)
Submission date: 2013-01-03
Abstract: We implement the Babylonian method to compute square roots of numbers. We provide precise algorithms for naturals, integers and rationals, and offer an approximation algorithm for linear ordered fields. Moreover, there are precise algorithms to compute the floor and the ceiling of the square root of an integer.
Change history: [2013-10-16]: Added algorithms to compute floor and ceiling of sqrt of integers.
