Possibilistic Noninterference

Title: Possibilistic Noninterference
Author: Andrei Popescu (uuomul /at/ yahoo /dot/ com) and Johannes Hölzl (hoelzl /at/ in /dot/ tum /dot/ de)
Submission date: 2012-09-10
Abstract: We formalize a wide variety of Volpano/Smith-style noninterference notions for a while language with parallel composition. We systematize and classify these notions according to compositionality w.r.t. the language constructs. Compositionality yields sound syntactic criteria (a.k.a. type systems) in a uniform way.

An article about these proofs is published in the proceedings of the conference Certified Programs and Proofs 2012.
