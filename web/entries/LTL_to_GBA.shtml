Title: Converting Linear-Time Temporal Logic to Generalized B端chi Automata
Author: Alexander Schimpf (schimpfa /at/ informatik /dot/ uni-freiburg /dot/ de) and Peter Lammich
Submission date: 2014-05-28
Abstract: We formalize linear-time temporal logic (LTL) and the algorithm by Gerth et al. to convert LTL formulas to generalized B端chi automata. We also formalize some syntactic rewrite rules that can be applied to optimize the LTL formula before conversion. Moreover, we integrate the Stuttering Equivalence AFP-Entry by Stefan Merz, adapting the lemma that next-free LTL formula cannot distinguish between stuttering equivalent runs to our setting.

We use the Isabelle Refinement and Collection framework, as well as the Autoref tool, to obtain a refined version of our algorithm, from which efficiently executable code can be extracted.
